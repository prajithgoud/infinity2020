About

A two day national level technology and career festival, providing a platform for one and all to witness the evolution of science and technology and to understand the advancement of technology with pure delight and enthusiasm.

We foster creativity and imagination to stimulate the professional superiority and proficiency. By providing a platform to showcase student science and art forms, Evolve –X creates a buzz of activity, driving students to reach their full artistic potential in an engaging, accessible environment.
